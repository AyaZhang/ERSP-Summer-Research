Instructions

The goal of our project is to classify products as either a “status good” or a “utility good”, and we are asking you to do this based on the product image, name, and price.

 

In general,

  • A status good is one that is bought with the aim of increasing one’s social desirability, or because the buyer sees the object as something attractive/desirable regardless of their need for the product’s functional use.
  • A utility good is one that is bought mainly because of its functionality, effectiveness, or usefulness.

You will judge 10 products in this HIT, with an additional question that is very simple and has verifiable answers. The question is included to support the validity of the other answers. Note that your submission may be rejected if you fail to answer that question. If some of the pictures have bad links ("loading"), please choose N/A. This may happen sometimes and it is not a problem.
